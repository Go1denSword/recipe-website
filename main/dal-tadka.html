Restaurant-Style Dal Tadka

Smooth, creamy yellow lentils tempered twice with ghee, garlic, cumin, and dry red chilies for that authentic smoky flavor.


Prep Time: 40 Mins Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Toor Dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1/4 cup Masoor Dal (red lentils)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • For the Tadka (Tempering):
  • 3 tbsp Ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 5-6 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 2-3 Dried Red Chilies
  • 1/2 tsp Kashmiri Red Chili Powder
  • 1 pinch Asafoetida (Hing)
  • Fresh Coriander leaves

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook the DalMushy hone take dal boil kro toor prefferebly
  2. Pan cooking Cook onions tamatoes etc then add yr mushy dal
  3. Tadka In small pan heat oil red chilli cumin
